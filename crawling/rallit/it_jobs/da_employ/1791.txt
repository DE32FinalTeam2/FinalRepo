오비맥주, 어떤 곳인가요?
ABInBev는 세계 1위 맥주 기업이자 세계 5대 소비재기업으로 500개가 넘는 브랜드를 포트폴리오를 보유한 글로벌 선두 기업입니다. AB InBev Korea 오비맥주는 카스, 한맥, OB라거, 필굿, 버드와이저, 스텔라, 호가든 등 소비자들의 입맛에 맞춘 차별화된 브랜드 포트폴리오로 국내 맥주시장을 선도하고 있습니다. 오비 맥주는 지속적인 혁신을 통해 새로운 제품과 더 강력한 브랜드를 만들고 있으며 고객 및 지역 사회와의 더욱 의미 있는 관계를 만들기 위해 노력합니다.

주요업무
Data Engineering Strategy: Develop and execute the data engineering strategy aligned with the organization's goals and objectives.
Data Infrastructure: Oversee the design, implementation, and maintenance of scalable and reliable data infrastructure, including data pipelines, storage systems, and data warehouses.
Data Quality and Governance: Establish and enforce data quality standards and governance policies to ensure the accuracy, completeness, and security of data.
Technical Expertise: Stay updated with emerging technologies, tools, and best practices in the data engineering field, and apply them to enhance data processing, integration, and analysis capabilities.
Team Leadership: Provide leadership and guidance to the data engineering team, ensuring efficient collaboration, effective communication, and a positive work environment.

[Leadership]
A mindset of continuous learning
Strategic thinking and the ability to align data engineering initiatives with organizational goals.
Problem-solving and decision-making skills to overcome challenges and drive successful outcomes
[Position Experience]

Over 8+ years of experience in data engineering, data architecture, or a related field.
Experience in managing and leading a team of data engineers.
Ability to effectively manage multiple projects and changing priorities
