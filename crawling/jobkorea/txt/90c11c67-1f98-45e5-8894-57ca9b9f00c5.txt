JOB DESCRIPTION
  Job Title                                                : Customer Support Representative
Region/ Country/ Function                 : APAC/ Korea/ Back Office - Logistics and CSR
  1.          Place of job in the organization structure
Reporting directly to the Operation Manager - Korea
  2.          PURPOSE OF THE JOB
A.         Commercial Part
·      To prepare quotations/handling the contract process/booking orders  
·      To Prepare invoice, delivery orders and all necessary documentation for execution of orders
·      To handle purchasing process dealing with 3rd parties and H.Q
  B.         Logistics Part
·      To arrange shipments for local and indent orders                                                                                             
·      To perform logistics coordination for repair to HQ
·      To manage export and import custom
·      To be responsible for stock Management
  C.         Customer Support Part
·      To receipt of the service requests from the customer and create the case via SFDC
·      To respond to customer calls and handle customer complaints
·      Administrative job on handling with the cost
·      Any other administrative task
  3.          KEY RESULTS AREAS
Timely invoice issued & Delivery
Well management of the customer requests
Support the customer support engineer’s requests
    4.          KNOWLEDGE AND EXPERTISE REQUIRED
Degree, Diploma, or equivalent in any discipline
Minimum 5 year’s relevant experience
Good communication skill both verbal and written in English.
Knowledge of MS office is a must
Preferred in good knowledge of ERP systems (SFDC/SAP)    
Ability to handle customer complaints
    5.          OTHER DUTIES
                Any other duties that may be assigned by the manager from time to time
