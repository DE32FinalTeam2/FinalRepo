Retail Development Specialist 채용
(경력 3년 이상/정규직)

트렉바이시클(Trek Bikes)는 1976년 미국 위스콘신에서 시작한 프리미엄 자전거 브랜드입니다.
트렉은 '우리는 우리가 사랑하는 최고의 제품만 만들고, 차별화된 고객 서비스를 제공하며,
많은 사람들이 자전거를 탈 수 있는 환경을 조성함으로써 세상의 변화를 주도한다.'라는 미션을 가지고 일합니다.
 트렉은 동료들을 웃게 만드는 사람을 원합니다.
일과 동료에게 진심인 사람들로 이루어진다면 진정으로 일하기 좋은 직장을 만들 수 있다고 믿습니다.
트렉의 미래는 바로 여러분에게 달려있습니다.
우리의 이야기에 공감사히는 분들은 지금 바로 트렉에 지원해주세요!
 트렉바이시클코리아는 2011년에 설립되어 자전거 및 자전거 악세사리 및 용품 판매 사업을 하고 있습니다.

모집부문 상세내용
Main Duties and
Responsibilities
As the Retail development specialist, you are responsible for the management of the store portfolio in South Korea, enabling long term growth by finding top locations for new stores, obtaining the best possible deals by negotiating lease contracts with property owners, active portfolio management of the existing portfolio, including optimizations, renegotiations, etc.
 At the same time, Develop consumer experience strategic plan for the market in line with global principles and guide country to ensure efficient implementation across DOTS and IBD stores. Responsible for the deployment of global concepts and the optimization of all consumer touchpoints to elevate consumer impact which lead to analyze and find out operation excellence of existing stores too.

The role of Retail development specialist is about internal and external collaboration, anticipating developments within our industry and taking into consideration how much the world changes all the time.
   - Enable growth and optimizing profitability for each brand by developing an up-to-date roadmap, understanding local customer behavior, setting ambitious targets, and negotiating deals professionally
- Analyze data, coordinate with other functions, complete due diligence on landlords, recommend business cases, and facilitate deals through the legal and construction process.
- Ensure the store portfolio is competitive, seeking improvements aligned with global guidelines, negotiating lease actions based on store performance, and maintaining up-to-date knowledge of landlords’ financial performance and asset changes
- Support key consultant of store construction budget across IBD and DOTS
- Support review architectural structure & permit issue with local engineer and architect to control construction process and overall timeline to meet target opening date.
- A solutions seeker with a ‘can do’ attitude.
- Direst own store customer data analysis support and find out key actions to fulfill operation successes.
Requirement
- At least 3+ years of working experience from leasing, controlling and/or real estate, preferably within the retail industry.
- 3+ years progressive retail operation and consumer experience expertise
- Leasing skills and good legal understanding.
- Strategic planning and long-term vision.
- Regional expertise desired
- Sporting and F&B Industry experience desired
- University degree ideally in Commerce, Real estate and international business
- MS Office, especially for Microsoft Excel use 
- Strong presentation, communication, negotiation and influencing skills.
- Strong cross-functional and stakeholder management experience across commercial functions and support functions.
- Need basic understanding and knowledge on structural, local code of architecture and market insight finding.
- Fluent Korean. Business communication level English is a plus.
- Willingness to travel upwards of 20% of time.
- Understanding of retail store operation and optimization.
- Basic analyzing skills of utilize store P&L and customer data for promotion implementation.
