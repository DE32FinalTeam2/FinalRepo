JOBPPLICATION ENGINEER FOR ASIA MARKET
  JOB Description
We are looking for an Application Engineer in the Asian Market. He/she will regionally represent the Product Management team for the Product Strategy set in the headquarters and will support the Asia Sales Team with the identification and growth of new business, as well as technical clarification and training for sales. He/she will observe competition landscape and business roadmaps of the main players for the strategic relevant markets. The position is for the Business Unit Materials and Components (particularly on Cores and Components) reporting in straight functional line to Sales Director, Asia and dotted line (administrative) to the VAC Korean Sales Manager.
JOB RESPONSIBILITIES:
Assumption of technical front-line to customer by working with sales regionally
Customer visits to support sales / account managers in (proactively) identifying technology-driven opportunities before a concrete opportunity is known
Responsible for understanding all applications of core markets where our solutions are beneficial
Be well versed with the product’s features, advantages, and benefits by application and to support technical case to convince customer in designing in our product .
Provision of technology / product trainings to sales / account managers
Attendance of fairs
Generation and documentation of market insights and new product ideas
  Assistance in technical versed inquiries / customers, maintenance of interface to New Product Introduction
Clarification of technical details in pre-acquisition phase
Provision of consultation for technical RFQ evaluation
Interfaces to Product Managers
Assume leadership in the introduction of new products designed for customers in coordination with R&D and Product Management at Headquarters
      JOB REQUIREMENTS:
University technical degree in Physics or Electrical or Electronics Engineering , solid knowledge in power electronics , optional in magnetic materials, and their applications
Prior experience in high technology segments, preferably within automotive or industry branches. Fresh graduate are welcome to apply .
Proven technical competent to understand new technologies.
Fluent in English, mother language Korean
Available for frequent travelling and social competence to meet with customers, including potential trips to Germany
Analytic, methodic and business oriented
Self-motivated and oriented to results. Willing to work in teams
  COMPENSATION PACKAGE & BENEFITS
We offer immediate incorporation to a (high reputation) technological leader company in specialty business (magnetics), with solid financial situation and plans to grow on a global footprint. Stable job and international environment, with long experience professional teams. Training in the headquarters will be done upon necessity.
Annual Performance Bonus
Leave entitlement
Employee Insurance Coverage (including medical, unemployment, work injury, pension)
5-day work week
