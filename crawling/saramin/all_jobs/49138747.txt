PDF 솔루션즈는 반도체 제조를 위한 첨단 분석 및 수율 개선 솔루션을 제공하는 글로벌 리더입니다. 당사의 Exensio® 플랫폼은 생산 공정 전반의 데이터를 통합 분석하여 품질과 신뢰성을 극대화하고, 머신러닝과 예측 분석을 통해 생산 효율성을 향상시킵니다. Industry 4.0과 클라우드 컴퓨팅 시대에 맞춰 반도체 제조업체들이 더 빠르고 정확한 의사 결정을 내릴 수 있도록 지원합니다.

PDF 솔루션즈의 DFI(Design-for-Inspection) 기술은 반도체 제조 공정에서 발생할 수 있는 전기적 결함을 조기에 식별하고, 생산 수율을 개선하기 위한 솔루션입니다. DFI는 공정 노드의 미세화를 지원하며, 기존 방법론보다 몇 달 앞서 결함을 찾아내는 것이 가능해 제조 과정의 품질과 효율성을 크게 향상시킵니다. 이를 통해 반도체 제조업체는 더 높은 수율과 제품 신뢰성을 확보할 수 있습니다

Semiconductor data analysis and consulting engineer

Job description

Main responsibilities are (but not limited to):
• Work with the client as well as the PDF engineering team to best determine the correct analysis techniques and to analyze the results to determine the best process for the client to implement.
• Act as the main interface point between the HQ Engineering team and onsite eBeam analysis support for different PDF clients.
• eBeam inspection data confirmation: Perform analysis of eBeam inspection results and ensure the quality inspection results worked as intended.
• Perform analysis that will include process splits, design rule analysis, process integration improvements, tool selection as well as other key critical requirements.
• Submit written analysis reports to the client based on PDF's data analysis tools and work with the client's physical failure analysis team to find root cause defects.
• Perform high quality semiconductor process characterization and to utilize this in combination with knowledge will result in client process improvements.
• Interface with the client daily to allow for a smooth transfer of information/knowledge/results. Finally, he will work with the client to further their understanding of PDF's tools and methodology.

Education and Experience

• The position requires a minimum of a bachelor’s Degree in Electrical Engineering or related field and at least three years of work experience in Semiconductor process integration, device engineering, DFM (Design For Manufacturing), YE(Yield Engineering), Metrology and Inspection on advanced logic (FinFET, GAA) and/or memory technologies.

• The candidate should be fluent in Korean and good at verbal and written English communication.

Preferred skills for the position
• Programming languages: Python
• Data analysis software: Spotfire, Exensio.
• Semiconductor layout drawing/viewing software.
• eBeam, SEM, TEM knowledges.
