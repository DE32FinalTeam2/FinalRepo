  [Position]
Manufacturing Engineer
  [SPECIFIC RESPONSIBILITIES]
• Develop manufacturing solutions in line with factory targets and product
reliability requirements.
• Set standard manufacturing practices and processes and independently
determine impact to design and parameters to the final products
• Work with leadership to design and build assembly line
• Be responsible for the manufacturability new product designs through
application of circuit diagram guidelines
• Lead circuit diagram review, co-work with cross-function team to evaluate and
manage manufacturing risk
• Conduct detailed circuit reviews to identify manufacturing pain points and
design alternatives.
• Product assembly SOP definition and review
• Partner with other engineering teams and quality teams to identify and
resolve manufacturing challenges by following best practices
• Review and provide feedback on product design for robustness and scaling.
• Provides production support engineering with respect to technically complex SMT procedures, testing methods and failure analysis.
• Present timely, concise reports advising the readiness of manufacturing equipment & processes
• Drive failure analysis and lead implementation of corrective actions.
  [EDUCATION AND EXPERIENCE REQUIREMENTS]
• Bachelor’s degree in an accredited course of study in engineering 
• 5+ years of experience in Electrical product design and/or manufacturing
• Experience with mass production of hardware products or related experience
• Experience in advanced analysis techniques including DOE, failure analysis,
PFMEA etc.
• Proficient with the use of Design for Manufacturing, SPC, DOE and Six Sigma
techniques
• Experience in production line development techniques including flow chart,
cycle time calculation, UPH setup, line layout & balance, SOP, etc.
• Strong analytical skills with an emphasis on data-driven decision making
• Experience collaborating with internal multi-discipline teams for mechanical,
electrical and firmware development, and external suppliers
• Experience understanding and challenging tolerance stack-ups to meet
gap/step requirements.
• Knowledge of quality control and part print references is also required.
• The ability to manage projects and work in a team project environment is
required.
• Working knowledge of quality improvement, lean manufacturing methods,
statistical process controls, and process capability
• A demonstrated history of accountability and performance delivery, leading
teams to meet or exceed objectives
• Fluent in English
