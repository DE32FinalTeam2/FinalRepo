- Work with Sales Managers and Service Leaders to support client needs and requests

Key Requirements
- Master's degree or above (engineering / science major)
- More than 10 years of application engineer or process engineer experience in the Semiconductor industry
- Experience of managing a team is preferred
- Business level of English communication skills
