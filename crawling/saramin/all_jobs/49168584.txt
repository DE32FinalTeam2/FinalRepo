- Manages customer requests, product escalations, and service emergencies
- Provide product support to new installations, and all active installed base

- Coordinate and support PUP and PIP activities at international sites as needed
- Coordinate RMA repair activities and prioritization based on customer needs
- Participate in new product definition and coordinate EHS requirements between Engineering and Customers
- Create and provide Product Training Documents and Procedures to field team
- Coordinate field customer quality and installation issue feedback
- Support process or quality related issues including periodical manufacturing process audit
- Leading the collection, summary, analysis, corrective actions, and reporting of data in alignment with Engineering, O- perations, and Sales.
- Create customer facing failure analysis or failure investigation progress reports
- Travel for training, support, and installation activities worldwide
- Support PO / PR activity using Oracle
- Other product support and customer support assignments as required

3-10 years of product support/service experience in the semiconductor test industry
