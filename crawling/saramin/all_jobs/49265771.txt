Demand Planner
Job Description
As the Demand Planner(Service Event Planner) you will create planned service events to maintain and upgrade customers lasers in Korea.  You will be responsible for ensuring the scope of work, parts availability and event timing align to result in seamless and well executed scanner laser service events
The Demand Planner (Service Event Planner) will create, manage and close all service events . This planner will work with local Field Service Engineers, Marketing, Reliability, Spares Inventory Planning and local Logistics Teams to develop tactical plans to ensure parts are available to meet customer need by dates.
 -          Execution of Synchronized Preventative Maintenance planning :  Including utilizing the planning tool data to create and service events, review weekly the schedule and status and maintain the planning with the mostly likely service date.
-          Determine the scope of work and which parts need to be replaced working closely with the field service engineer.
-          Provide post-event review with respect to customer satisfaction, event quality and cost.
-          Summarize and communicate to San Diego management any parts shortage and provide clear ultimate need dates.
-          Manage the implementation of new products to meet implementation strategy timeline and objectives. Ensure that parts orders and usage is aligned with factory allocation timelines to ensure events opened have parts on-hand for execution.
-          Evaluate and solution slow moving and excess inventory.
-          Monitor Korea internal factory output and minimize logistics costs between San Diego and Korea allocations.
-          Other duties as assigned
 Education / Experience
-          Bachelor’s degree in engineering or business with 10+ years of relevant work experience.
-          3+ years of experience in semiconductor fabrication and / or semiconductor capital equipment
-          Ability to work independently and within a cross functional team of engineers, account managers, logistics and the customer representatives

Personal Skills
-          Able to operate with autonomy to ensure SEP Planning goals, guidelines and systems are in place to support execution of Cymer’s service program.
-          Ability to differentiate between “wants” and “needs” and challenge with data.
-          Proficient use of all Microsoft Office suite programs
-          ERP planning – Oracle preferred
-          Demantra knowledge preferred
-          Past experience with field service or production planning preferred
 Main Work location: 평택 (ASML Cymer Korea)
 Other information

-          Occasionally lift and/or move up to 20 pounds.
-          May require travel domestic and/or international dependent on business needs – less than 10%
