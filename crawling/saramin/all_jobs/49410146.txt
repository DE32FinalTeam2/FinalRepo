Main focus for this position is the OVALiS and 5DA product. 
Be in charge of the projects related to new product introduction: definition, characterization, alpha testing, 
and beta testing. 
Be responsible for various pre and post sales application support activities – engaging with customers in 
defining problem statements, resolving customer issues, generating best known method (BKM) documents and other training material, and transferring product knowledge to company's worldwide customer base.
Work with a customer team at customer sites to conduct investigations, gather and analyze data, 
understand root causes of process patterning errors, and develop and implement process tool control 
solutions to reduce patterning errors and improve yield.
Work with customers to publish papers at industry symposiums or in technical journals.
Hands-on work with company metrology systems and software will be required.
Process tool or applications experience with intimate familiarity in overlay, stress related error, 
scanner tools, lithography or etch metrology tooling and/or process development is plus.
