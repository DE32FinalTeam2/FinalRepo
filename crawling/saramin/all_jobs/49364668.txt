[Summary]
Janssen Vaccines is recruiting for an Engineer, Execution Systems, Incheon Site, Large Molecule Platform. This position must be based at an Incheon (Janssen Vaccines) site.
The Execution Systems is managing all of the systems in MTO (Manufacturing Technology and Operation) including Process Control System, Building Management System, Environmental Management System, Utility Management System, Automation Integration layer, PI system.
The Engineer will report to the Execution Systems Site lead. The Position at site level will be responsible for implementing, supporting and maintaining the ISA-95 level 1, 2 and 3 systems. Site ES engineer will execute all the activities required to ensure that computerized systems are fit for intended use and compliant with applicable regulations throughout the entire system life cycle
Site ES engineer will be responsible for maintaining and updating local recipe elements (process content, technology process actions and phases, libraries) and CSV & Data Integrity as Manufacturing System Administrator and maintain Network and Virtual machine servers that possess L2 Systems.
