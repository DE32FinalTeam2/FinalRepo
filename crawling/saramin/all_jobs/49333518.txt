[인테그리스코리아]
Field Service Engineer 채용
Entegris is seeking a Field Service Engineer, material solutions division who will have tremendous visibility within the company, leading efforts to solve our customers’ most challenging problems and sell customer service. The Field Service Engineer will be assisting with liquid, solid, and gas equipment commissioning, troubleshooting, liquid / gas / solid canister changes, data collection, and facilitating prompt return of empty cylinders and canisters. Training will be provided to help the candidate increase competency and progress in the company.
인테그리스는 회사 내에서 거대한 가시성을 확보하고, 고객의 가장 어려운 문제를 해결하고, 고객 서비스를 판매하기 위한 노력을 주도할 Material Solution 부서인 Field Service Engineer를 찾고 있습니다. Field Service Engineer는 액체, 고체 및 가스 장비 시운전, 문제 해결, 액체/가스/고체 캐니스터 변경, 데이터 수집, 빈 실린더 및 캐니스터의 신속한 반환 업무를 지원할 예정입니다. 지원자가 회사 내 역량을 높이고 발전할 수 있도록 교육이 제공될 예정입니다.

* 사업확장으로 인한 채용입니다.




In this role you will
l  Conduct solid source delivery cabinet (SSDC) or liquid delivery system (LDS) shipment,
installation, set-up, troubleshooting, and maintenance for multiple customers
l  Serve hardware parts replacement and software upgrade services for customer requests and
development / improvement plan
l  Perform ampoule / canister / cylinder replacement work based on SOP. The replacement work
includes a leak check and pressure monitoring, etc..
l  When handling chemicals, be sure to study and understand the Material Safety Data Sheet (MSDS)
l  Own customer evaluations to ensure sharp execution so benefits of the results are aligned with
customer buying motives and presented in a professional manner
l  Need to minimize customer complaint through smooth communication and quick response
l  Communicate without barriers with account teams, supply chain, business units, and R&D teams
in case of technical issues and to work efficiently.
l  Train customers and fellow colleagues on relevant technical knowledge
 
Education/Experience
l  3 - 5 years semiconductor field support / service experience is required
l  Bachelor’s degree in electrical / electronic / mechanical / material / chemical engineering is required
l  Smooth communication skill is STRONGLY required
l  Experience with vacuum valve, pressure gauge, MFC, regulator, fitting, switch, etc.. is a plus
l  Experience with electrical controls, PLC modules, relays, etc.. is a plus
l  Excellent analytical and organizational skills, including the proven ability to adapt to a dynamic work
environment and manage multiple activities
l  Proficient with Microsoft Office (Word, Excel, PowerPoint, MS Project) is a plus
l  Proficient in written and verbal English is a plus
