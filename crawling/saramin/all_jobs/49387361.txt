1. Summary
- Responsible for design and development of optical products for 112G signaling and beyond.
- The individual will be responsible for developing active optical cable and optical transceiver products and supporting
the development of test systems and product qualifications.
- The position requires close customer interaction with the development of product requirements and specifications.
- The engineering team helps define the roadmap and strategy for fast-growing businesses in cloud computing and large
datacenter deployments.

2. Responsibilities
- Design, develop, debug, and support active optical cables and optical transceiver products at 200G, 400G, 800 G and beyond.
- Lead the schematic and PCBA design for active optical cable and transceiver products.
- Establish product testing procedure; Identify test requirement and equipment for optical product characterizations.
- Work hand-in-hand with firmware engineer in the development of active optical cable and transceiver products.
- Work with test engineer/technician on Engineering Verification Tests (EVT) and Design Verification Tests (DVT).
- Work with NPI and process engineers on new product introduction to manufacturing sites.
- Work with mechanical integrity team and mechanical design team in the development of active optical cable and
transceiver products.

3. Preferred Additional Experiences
- Work experience in multi-disciplinary engineering teams.
- Knowledge of system level and channel/link modeling.
- Good understanding of optical transceiver technical evolution.
- Good time management skills.
- Strong written and verbal communication skills.
- 10 years or more work experiences
